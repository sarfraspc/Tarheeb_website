About Tarheeb

With over 25 years of excellence, Tarheeb has established itself as UAE's most trusted partner for comprehensive immigration and documentation solutions. Our team of licensed professionals offers specialized expertise across a wide array of services, including company formation, trade licenses, and all types of visa processing—encompassing employment, family, investor, and Golden Visas. We also provide essential Tasheel & Labor Services, along with efficient attestation and translation for critical documents.

At Tarheeb, we are dedicated to ensuring a seamless and efficient experience for all our clients. We prioritize complete transparency throughout every process, from visa applications to document attestation and business setup services. Our commitment to professional excellence and guaranteed results has made us the preferred choice for individuals and businesses navigating UAE's regulatory landscape.
